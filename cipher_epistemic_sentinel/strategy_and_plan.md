By establishing the CIPHER framework, we create a strict environment where the AI acts as a constraint engine that forces the Human to achieve epistemic authority. 3. **Symbolic Scars:** We will utilize a local schema (e.g., `SymbolicScar.json`) to track and ingest failure topologies, implementing a primitive but effective "Autopoietic Composting" mechanism.
